[b]IMPORTANT: This is the entire shared assets of the Call of Duty: Black Ops III - Weapons mod. It's only required if you either subscribed to the Call of Duty: Black Ops III - Weapons or another mod that depends on this mod for the attachments or material shaders.[/b]

This mod simply contains materials and some attachments for the Black Ops III weapon mod or any other mod that wants to take advantage of the custom shaders for their models. This is required by the Black Ops III weapon mod or any other future mod that I or other people that needs this mod for the materials or attachments.

Also, this mod doubles as a modders resource. See the threads on more info on the extended materials and compiling your mod with this mod.

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=http://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1132835147/1474222595306563689/]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=http://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1132835147/1474222595306563142/]General Feedback Thread[/url].
[B]If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.[/B]

I don't ask for donations. Period.
