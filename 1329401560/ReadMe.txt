This mod adds three sneaking suits that can be worn by both human males and females for all units: The MSF Sneaking Suit, the CCCP Sneaking Suit and the Boss' Sneaking Suit, all from Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain (they were DLC for that game). In addition to that, this mod also adds Boss' Bandana and Big Boss' eyepatch that both males and females can wear.
