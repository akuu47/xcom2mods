[b]DO NOT SKIP THE REQUIRED ITEMS! THEY ARE NECESSARY!![/b]

[b]Alien Hunters DLC Icon, Name Support and Empty Arms and Legs [WOTC] is required if you plan on customizing your Alien Hunter armors too.[/b]

This mod adds 2 scout uniforms that can be worn by both human males and females for all units: The LRR Concealment Uniform and Ghillie Suit from Metal Gear Online 3. In addition to that, this mod also adds several helmets from MGO3 that both males and females can wear.

[h1][b]VANILLA VERSION WILL COME SOON![/b][/h1]

[b]If anyone wants to help port the hero or any other uniforms, or accelerate the rate that this mod gets more content, feel free to get in touch with me. I really just need more skinners that are able to rig uniforms to XCOM 2.[/b]

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1392996834/1696048245845031606/]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1392996834/1696048245845033132/]General Feedback Thread[/url].

If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.

I don't ask for donations. Period.
