This mod adds 4 heavy armors that can be worn by both human males and females for all units: MGO3 Enforcer Armor, Diamond Dogs' Battledress, PMC Riot Suit, and the Soviet Heavy Armor, all from Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain and Metal Gear Online 3. In addition to that, this mod also adds several helmets from MGO3 that both males and females can wear.

[h1][b]VANILLA VERSION WILL COME SOON![/b][/h1]

[b]Alien Hunters DLC Icon, Name Support and Empty Arms and Legs [WOTC] is required if you plan on customizing your Alien Hunter armors too.[/b]

[b]If anyone wants to help port the hero or any other uniforms, or accelerate the rate that this mod gets more content, feel free to get in touch with me. I really just need more skinners that are able to rig uniforms to XCOM 2.[/b]

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1368395306/3211505894142555266/]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1368395306/3211505894142556789/]General Feedback Thread[/url].

If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.
