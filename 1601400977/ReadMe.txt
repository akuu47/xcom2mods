Feelin' a little down that War of the Chosen or Jake's mod (TLP) is lacking poses or desperate for even more poses? Then this mod is for you! This mod adds 97 new poses to the Photobooth handcrafted by members of our community.

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1601400977/1744480967003280659/]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1601400977/1744480967003281757/]General Feedback Thread[/url].

If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.

I don't ask for donations. Period.

[h1]ABOUT CONTENT UPDATES[/h1]
[h1]-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.[/h1]
For starters, Year Zero [0] is the initial release of the pose pack. Each major content update to the pose pack will be done in Seasons. You can request a pose and maybe it will be implemented in the next season update. If you're really desperate for a pose, then I suggest doing it yourself.
After Year 0, the Seasons goes up by 1, starting at Season 1. There is no deadline of the release of the next season as we like to work at our own pace.
