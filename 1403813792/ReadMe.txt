This mod adds headgear worn by Diamond Dogs and 3 sets of uniforms from Diamond Dogs and Charon Corps (from Metal Gear: Survive): The BDUs seen in the end of Cloaked In Silence, the Fatigues seen around Mother Base and in other cutscenes, and Uniforms worn by the Engineer and other survivors in Metal Gear: Survive. Each set of uniforms is available to wear for both males and females of all classes.

[quote]If anyone wants to help port other uniforms, or accelerate the rate of more content, feel free to get in touch with me. I really just need more skinners that are able to rig uniforms to XCOM 2.[/quote]

If you have issues with this mod or spotted a bug, please report it in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1403813792/1697175413667785351/]Bug Report Thread[/url]. If you want to provide feedback, whether its good or bad, then post in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/1403813792/1697175413667787015/]General Feedback Thread[/url].

If you are thinking of downvoting this mod, please provide feedback first, so I know what I should improve on/did wrong.

I don't ask for donations. Period.
